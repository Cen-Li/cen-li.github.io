To familiarize yourself with executing a Karel program, try running the maze executable located in $PUB. Issue the command     % $PUB/maze $PUB/maze.wor
(Remember, do NOT enter the percent sign as part of your command. It is meant to represent the prompt provided by the system.) If all went well, you should have a screen that looks something like the following:

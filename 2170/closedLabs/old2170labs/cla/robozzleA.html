RoboZZle Lab A

In this lab, you will learn the ideas of recursive function using the RoboZZle game. Robozzle does NOT run on Ranger. So you will need to do this assignment on the CSCI lab window systems.
  1. First, finish the RoboZZle tutorial at http://www.robozzle.com/;
  2. Then, use what you learned from the tutorial to solve this puzzle: http://www.robozzle.com/index.aspx?puzzle=68. Once you solve the puzzle, a pop up window will appear asking if you want "More puzzles" or "Replay" Click the "Replay". This will show the solved puzzle plus your program on screen. Now print from your browser. Turn in this printed hardcopy of the solved puzzle. Write your name on the hardcopy print out and turn it in to the lab assistant.
